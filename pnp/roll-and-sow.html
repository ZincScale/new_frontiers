Roll & Sow Quick Reference

1-4 players • playtest edition

Setup

  1. Reveal players + 2 six-cost goals; mark 2 candidates each.
  2. Then use normal Faction and Home World setup.
  3. Roll 3 White dice into their face bowls.
  4. Put 2 White dice in Citizenry.
  5. Start with 1 Credit and one build of each type.
  6. VP pool: 7 chips per player.

Round

  1. Secretly select one nonempty bowl.
  2. Reveal; add the 2p supply-die phase.
  3. Resolve selected phases I → V.
  4. Everyone activates that phase's bowl.
  5. Spend any dice; sow every unused die.
  6. Manage Empire: recruit; Recall 1 if all bowls empty; check goals.
Activate the bowl first. Use dice as costs. Sow every die you do not use, starting in the next bowl clockwise.

Phase Actions And Used-Die Destinations

Explore
Spend N: inspect N+3 tiles; keep 1. Or spend 1 to Stock for 2 Credits or Claim an extra goal.
Citizenry
Develop
Each die adds 1 permanent progress to any Development. Multiple builds may complete.
On build → Citizenry
Settle
Each die adds 1 permanent progress to any World. All Worlds use printed cost.
On build → Citizenry
Produce
Each die becomes a Good on an empty non-gray production World.
World as Good
Ship
Each die ships 1 Good: Trade for 3/4/5/6 Credits or Consume for 1 VP.
Both → Citizenry

Sowing

  • First unused die goes in the next phase bowl.
  • Continue clockwise, one die per bowl.
  • Wrap around as often as needed.
  • Choose the order of differently colored dice.
  • A die sown into a later selected bowl acts this round.

Printed Die Locations

Cup gain
Roll now into its face bowl.
Citizenry gain
Recruit later for 1 Credit.
World gain
Place as a Good; do not roll.
Wild face
Choose any bowl.
Reassign
Move a legal die before reveal.

Explore Discard Cycle

  • Draw physical tiles and inspect both sides.
  • Keep one chosen side under construction.
  • Discard every rejected tile face down.
  • When the bag runs short, shuffle discards into it.
  • Maximum: 3 Developments and 3 Worlds queued.

Tile Powers

  • Use normal text when its concept still exists.
  • Extra workers are virtual and are never sown.
  • Cost reductions reduce progress required.
  • Phase-end powers trigger whenever the phase occurs.
  • Backup Planning = one unrestricted Reassign.

Provisional Solo

  • Use a 24-chip pool and a 20-round limit.
  • Select one nonempty bowl.
  • Reveal one card from a five-phase deck.
  • Both phases occur; matching phases occur once.
  • Reshuffle after all five cards are seen.

Goal Commitment

  • Trigger: half the VP pool gone or anyone completes 6 tiles.
  • Commit 1 candidate; later claimed goals commit immediately.
  • Fulfill the goal minimum to score its printed bonus conversion.
  • Miss the minimum: -6 VP per committed goal.
  • Goals grant no phase power, face VP, or tableau square.
  • End at 12 tableau squares or empty VP pool; score normal VP + goals.

Worked Example

  1. Mira selects Develop; Sol selects Settle.
  2. Develop: Mira uses 1 of 3 dice, then sows 2 into Settle and Produce.
  3. Sol sows his unused Develop die into Settle.
  4. Settle: newly sown dice join dice already there and may act now.
  5. Produce was not selected, so dice sown there wait.
