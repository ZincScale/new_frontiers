Round Sequence

Reference
  1. Select one phase secretly.
  2. Reveal selected phases.
  3. Resolve selected phases in order.
  4. Manage Empire: spend Credits to recharge.

No Roll Dice step. No Assign Dice step. Multiplayer VP pool: 16/player.

Spend / Build

Reference
  • Blue: Explore worker.
  • Brown/Red: pay Develop/Settle costs.
  • Green: Produce worker.
  • Purple: Ship worker.
  • White: any selected phase.
  • Develop/Settle must be paid in one shot.

Gain Dice

Reference

When a tile says to gain a die, pip up that color.

  1. Increase max by 1, max 6.
  2. Increase current by 1, not above max.
  3. Excess beyond 6 is lost.

Green 0/2 + Genes die = Green 1/3.

Credits

Reference
  • Credits do not recruit dice.
  • Spend Credits with pips to complete Develop/Settle.
  • Partial Develop/Settle progress is not kept.
  • Spend 1 Credit to recharge 1 current pip.
  • Never recharge above max.

Produce / Ship

Reference
  • Produce: place a Good marker, not a worker die.
  • The Good color is the pip color spent.
  • Ship: spend Purple or White to Trade or Consume.
  • White Goods count as matching.

Alien Yellow

Reference
  • Yellow starts 0/0.
  • Gain Alien Technology die: pip up Yellow.
  • Yellow pays only for Alien Develop, Settle, Produce, or Ship work.
  • Alternative: Yellow as extra Ship capacity.

Tile Powers

Reference
  • Ignore reassign powers.
  • Gain/recruit/place die: pip up that color.
  • Extra worker: gain 1 temporary pip for that phase.
  • Cost reduction: reduce pips/Credits required.

End Game

Reference
  • VP pool exhausted.
  • Any player reaches 12 tableau squares.
  • Use 16 VP chips per player for multiplayer.
  • More VP chips only lengthens VP-pool endings.
  • Normal VP chips and tile VP.
  • Specific-color dice bonus: count that color's max pips.

Solo Setup

Solo
  1. Use one normal player setup.
  2. Use a 30 VP chip pool.
  3. Set Dummy Goods to 0.
  4. Shuffle 5 Dummy phase cards.
  5. Choose one campaign sheet.
  6. Play exactly 12 rounds.

Condition Key 1

Solo
  • Builder: 31+ VP and complete 7+ tiles.
  • Developer: 31+ VP and have 4+ Developments.
  • Colonizer: 31+ VP and have 6+ Worlds.
  • Satisfied Populace: 31+ VP and score 10+ VP chips.
  • Industrial: 31+ VP and have 16+ total max pips.

Condition Key 2

Solo
  • Production: 31+ VP and have 4+ production Worlds.
  • Diverse: 31+ VP and have 3+ World colors.
  • Novelty: 31+ VP and have 2+ Novelty Worlds.
  • Rare Elements: 31+ VP and have 2+ Rare Worlds.
  • Alien Contact: 31+ VP and have 1+ Alien World.
  • Military: 31+ VP and Red max 5.
  • Discovery: 31+ VP and Blue max 5.

Campaign Play

Solo
  1. Play 4 games in a row.
  2. After each game, mark 1 satisfied unmarked scenario.
  3. If you cannot mark a new scenario, lose the campaign.
  4. Mark 4 different scenarios to win.

Solo Round

Solo
  1. Select your phase.
  2. Reveal 2 Dummy phase cards.
  3. Resolve all selected phases in order.
  4. Resolve both Dummy card effects.
  5. Manage Empire normally.

Dummy Effects

Solo
  • Explore: claim 1 Development and 1 World.
  • Develop: claim 1 Development.
  • Settle: claim 1 World.
  • Produce: Dummy Goods +1, max 4.
  • Ship: drain 2 VP chips per Dummy Good, minimum 2.

Solo End

Solo
  • Round limit is reached.
  • VP chip pool is exhausted.
  • Your tableau reaches 12 squares.
  • Finish the round, then mark 1 campaign scenario.
