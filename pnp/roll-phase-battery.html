Phase Battery Reference

Multiplayer Rules

Round Sequence

  1. Select one phase secretly.
  2. Reveal selected phases.
  3. Resolve selected phases in order.
  4. Manage Empire: spend Credits to recharge.

No Roll Dice or Assign Dice step. VP pool: 8/player at 2p, 12/player at 3p+.

Spend Pips

Blue
Explore worker.
Brown
Develop build currency.
Red
Military level; not spent.
Green
Produce worker.
Purple
Ship worker.
White
Credit track.

Explore

  1. Scout: spend 1+ Explore pips to search that many candidate tiles of one type.
  2. Keep 1 tile and put it on the bottom of that build stack.
  3. If both build stacks already have tiles, each Explore pip may Stock for +2 Credits.

Explore is the tempo-cost escape hatch: pips buy search depth or money, but not direct tableau progress.

Develop / Settle

  • Pay the top tile's full cost in one shot.
  • Develop uses Brown pips and/or Credits.
  • Military Worlds require Red level at least equal to cost; Red does not spend down.
  • Normal Worlds use Credits, plus eligible Yellow for Alien Worlds.
  • If you cannot pay the full cost, spend nothing and keep the tile queued.
  • On completion, move the tile to tableau and apply converted effects.

Credits / Recharge

  • Credits do not recruit dice.
  • Credits pay Develop and normal Settle costs.
  • Spend 1 Credit to recharge 1 current pip during Manage Empire.
  • Never recharge Red, White, or above max.
  • If your Credit marker is at 0 after Manage Empire, set it to 1.

Gain Dice

  1. Increase that color's max by 1, max 6.
  2. Increase current by 1, not above max.
  3. Excess beyond 6 is lost.

Green 0/2 + Genes die = Green 1/3.

Produce / Ship

  • Produce: place a Good marker, not a worker die.
  • The Good color is the pip color spent.
  • Ship: spend Purple to Trade or Consume.
  • Yellow can Ship Alien Goods in Alien mode.

Alien Yellow

  • Yellow starts 0/0.
  • Gain Alien Technology die: pip up Yellow.
  • Yellow pays only for Alien Develop, Settle, Produce, or Ship work.
  • Alternative: Yellow as extra Ship capacity.

Tile Powers / End Game

  • Ignore reassign powers.
  • Gain/recruit/place die: pip up that color.
  • Extra worker: gain 1 temporary pip for that phase.
  • Cost reduction: reduce pips/Credits required.
  • Game ends when VP pool is exhausted or a player reaches 12 tableau squares.
  • 6-cost bonuses use converted battery scoring.
  • White counts only as Credits, not owned dice, unless a bonus says Credits.

Solo Guide And Campaigns

12-Round Challenge

Solo Setup

  1. Use one normal player setup.
  2. Use a 30 VP chip pool.
  3. Set Dummy Goods to 0.
  4. Shuffle 5 Dummy phase cards.
  5. Choose one campaign sheet.
  6. Play exactly 12 rounds.

Solo Round

  1. Select your phase.
  2. Reveal 2 Dummy phase cards.
  3. Resolve all selected phases in order.
  4. Resolve both Dummy card effects.
  5. Manage Empire normally.

Campaign Play

  1. Play 4 games in a row.
  2. After each game, mark 1 satisfied unmarked condition.
  3. If you cannot mark a new condition, lose the campaign.
  4. Mark all 4 conditions to win.

Named Conditions

  • Builder: Named VP and complete 8+ tiles.
  • Developer: Named VP and have 5+ Developments.
  • Colonizer: Named VP and have 6+ Worlds.
  • Satisfied Populace: Named VP and score 11+ VP chips.
  • Industrial: Named VP and difficulty's max-pip target.
  • Production: Named VP and have 4+ production Worlds.
  • Diverse: Named VP and have 4+ World colors.
  • Novelty: Named VP and have 2+ Novelty Worlds.
  • Rare Elements: Named VP and have 2+ Rare Worlds.
  • Alien Contact: Named VP and have 1+ Alien World.
  • Military: Named VP and Red level 4.
  • Discovery: Named VP and Blue max 4.

Dummy Effects

  • Explore: claim 1 Development and 1 World.
  • Develop: claim 1 Development.
  • Settle: claim 1 World.
  • Produce: Dummy Goods +1, max 4.
  • Ship: drain 2 VP chips per Dummy Good, minimum 2.

Solo End

  • Round limit is reached.
  • VP chip pool is exhausted.
  • Your tableau reaches 12 squares.
  • Finish the round, then mark 1 campaign condition.
