Phase Battery Reference

Multiplayer Rules

Round Sequence

  1. Solo/2p: select 2 phases. 3p+: select 1 phase.
  2. Each selected phase needs at least 1 ready pip.
  3. Phase selection does not spend pips.
  4. Resolve selected phases: Explore, Develop, Settle, Produce, Ship.
  5. During each phase, players act in turn order.
  6. Spend usable pips or pass.
  7. Manage Empire: spend Credits to recharge.

No Roll Dice or Assign Dice step. Score from tableau VP, VP chips, and chosen 6-cost goals.

Explore

  1. Scout: spend 1+ Explore pips to search that many candidate tiles of one type.
  2. Keep 1 tile and put it in the matching construction area.
  3. If both construction areas are full, each Explore pip may Stock for +2 Credits.

Explore is the tempo-cost escape hatch: pips buy search depth or money, but not direct tableau progress.

Develop / Settle

  • Develop pips may stay as progress on any Development.
  • You may complete multiple Developments in one Develop phase.
  • Credits are not wild Develop workers by default.
  • Military Worlds require enough Red, then exhaust 1 current Red.
  • Normal Worlds use White, plus eligible Yellow for Alien Worlds.
  • You may complete multiple Worlds in one Settle phase.
  • Normal Worlds may keep Settle progress like Developments.
  • On completion, move the tile to tableau and apply converted effects.

Credits / Recharge

  • Credits do not recruit dice.
  • Credits are not wild workers by default.
  • Trade and some tile effects gain Credits.
  • Spend 1 Credit to recharge 1 current pip during Manage Empire.
  • Never recharge above max.
  • If your Credit count is 0 after Manage Empire, set it to 1.

Gain Dice

  1. Increase that color's max by 1, max 6.
  2. Increase current by 1, not above max.
  3. Excess beyond 6 is lost.

Green 0/2 + Genes die = Green 1/3.

Produce / Ship

  • Produce: place a Good marker, not a worker die.
  • The Good color is the pip color spent.
  • Ship: spend Purple to Trade for Credits or Consume for VP chips.
  • Trade value: Blue 3, Brown 4, Green 5, Yellow 6.
  • Consume value: base 1 VP, plus converted matching bonuses.
  • Yellow can Ship Alien Goods in Alien mode.

Alien Yellow

  • Yellow starts 0/0.
  • Gain Alien Technology die: pip up Yellow.
  • Yellow pays only for Alien Develop, Settle, Produce, or Ship work.
  • Alternative: Yellow as extra Ship capacity.

Tile Powers / End Game

  • Ignore reassign powers.
  • Gain/recruit/place die: pip up that color.
  • Extra worker: gain 1 temporary pip for that phase.
  • Cost reduction: reduce pips required.
  • Game ends when a player reaches 12 tableau squares or the VP pool empties.
  • 6-cost Developments are delayed end-game goals.
  • Goals tie to named solo conditions: Novelty, Populace, Alien, Developer, Builder, Industrial, Rare, Production, Military, Diverse.
  • Miss a committed goal's minimum: -6 VP.
  • White settles non-Military Worlds; Credits are unlimited chips.

Solo Guide And Campaigns

15-Round Challenge

Solo Setup

  1. Use one normal player setup.
  2. Set Dummy Goods to 0.
  3. Shuffle 5 Dummy phase cards.
  4. Choose one campaign sheet.
  5. Use a 24 VP-chip pool: you + Dummy.
  6. Play exactly 15 rounds.

Solo Round

  1. Select 2 phases, each with at least 1 ready pip.
  2. Phase selection does not spend pips.
  3. Reveal 2 Dummy phase cards.
  4. Resolve selected phases in normal order.
  5. Spend usable pips or pass.
  6. Resolve Dummy effects for Dummy phases.
  7. Manage Empire, then check 6-cost goal commit.

Campaign Play

  1. Play 4 games in a row.
  2. After each game, mark 1 satisfied unmarked condition.
  3. If you cannot mark a new condition, lose the campaign.
  4. Mark all 4 conditions to win.

Named Conditions

  • Builder: Named VP and complete 8+ tiles.
  • Developer: Named VP and have 4+ Developments.
  • Colonizer: Named VP and have 5+ Worlds.
  • Satisfied Populace: Named VP and ship 4+ Goods.
  • Industrial: Named VP and difficulty's max-pip target.
  • Production: Named VP and have 4+ production Worlds.
  • Diverse: Named VP and have 4+ World colors.
  • Novelty: Named VP and have 2+ Novelty Worlds.
  • Rare Elements: Named VP and have 2+ Rare Worlds.
  • Alien Contact: Named VP and have 1+ Alien World.
  • Military: Named VP and Red 5.
  • Discovery: Named VP and Blue max 5.

Dummy Effects

  • Explore: claim 1 Development and 1 World.
  • Develop: claim 1 Development.
  • Settle: claim 1 World.
  • Produce: Dummy Goods +1, max 4.
  • Ship: set Dummy Goods to 0.

Solo End

  • Round limit is reached.
  • Your tableau reaches 12 squares.
  • Goals tie to named solo conditions: Novelty, Populace, Alien, Developer, Builder, Industrial, Rare, Production, Military, Diverse.
  • Miss a committed 6-cost goal's minimum: -6 VP.
  • Finish the round, then mark 1 campaign condition.
