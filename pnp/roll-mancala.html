Round Sequence

Reference
  1. Choose a board section or one Spent color group.
  2. Reveal choices and sow.
  3. Final landing section selects your phase.
  4. Resolve selected phases in Roll order.
  5. Manage Empire and buy recovery sows.

No Roll Dice step. No Assign Dice step. VP pool: 11/player.

Sow Board

Reference
  1. Pick up all dice from one section.
  2. Choose their order.
  3. Sow clockwise, one die per section.
  4. Skip full sections.
  5. The final landing section selects your phase.

Explore -> Develop -> Settle -> Produce -> Ship.

Sow Spent

Reference
  1. Choose one color group in Spent.
  2. Blue enters Explore.
  3. Brown enters Develop.
  4. Red enters Settle.
  5. Green enters Produce.
  6. Purple enters Ship.
  7. White/yellow choose any normal section.

Use Dice

Reference
  • Dice work only in their current section's phase.
  • White and yellow can sit in any normal phase section.
  • Develop/Settle workers stay as progress.
  • Produce workers become Goods on Worlds.
  • Explore/Ship workers go to Spent after use.

Match Bonuses

Reference

Explore: draw +1 tile before choosing.

Develop: reduce one cost by 1.

Settle: reduce one cost by 1.

Produce: make one extra eligible Good.

Ship: +1 VP on first Consume.

Check only the final die of each sow.

Gain / Lose

Reference
  • Gain a die: add it to its matching section.
  • White and yellow gains go to Spent.
  • If matching section is full, gain to Spent.
  • Lose a die: remove that physical die.
  • Capacity is the dice you own.

Builds

Reference
  • Keep one Development and one World under construction.
  • Workers on builds are lasting progress.
  • Credits are paid only when completing.
  • Completed build workers move to Spent.
  • Abandon one build during Manage Empire.

Credits

Reference
  • Credits never become dice.
  • Spend Credits to finish Develop/Settle.
  • During Manage Empire, spend 2 Credits for a recovery sow.
  • Recovery sows move one Spent color group.
  • Recovered dice are ready next round.

End Game

Reference
  • VP pool exhausted.
  • Any player reaches 12 tableau squares.
  • Normal tile VP plus VP chips.
  • Specific-color dice bonuses count owned physical dice.
  • Use 11 VP chips per player for multiplayer.

Solo Setup

Solo
  1. Use one mancala player setup.
  2. Use a 30 VP chip pool.
  3. Set Dummy Goods to 0.
  4. Shuffle 5 Dummy phase cards.
  5. Choose one campaign sheet.
  6. Play up to 16 rounds.

Solo Round

Solo
  1. Choose and sow one source.
  2. Reveal 2 Dummy phase cards.
  3. Resolve all selected phases in order.
  4. Resolve both Dummy effects.
  5. Manage Empire normally.

Dummy Effects

Solo
  • Explore: claim 1 Development and 1 World.
  • Develop: claim 1 Development.
  • Settle: claim 1 World.
  • Produce: Dummy Goods +1, max 4.
  • Ship: drain 2 VP per Dummy Good, minimum 2.

Tile Text

Guide
  • Use normal Roll meaning first.
  • Translate dice into physical mancala pieces.
  • No Dice Cup, Citizenry, Wild phase, or Alien phase.
  • Sections decide what work a die can do now.
  • Color remains identity for tile text and scoring.

Green to Produce, or Spent if full.

Purple to Ship, or Spent if full.

Gain Flex

Guide
  • Gain Alien Technology die: yellow to Spent.
  • Gain white die: white to Spent.
  • When recovered, choose any normal phase entry section.
  • Yellow and white work only in their current section's phase.
  • Neither color has its own mancala phase.

Lose Dice

Guide
  • Lose Military die: remove one red die.
  • Lose Novelty die: remove one blue die.
  • Lose any die: choose one physical die owned.
  • Default priority: Spent, section, Good, construction.
  • If you do not own that color, ignore the loss.

Extra Worker

Guide
  • Temporary workers do not become owned dice.
  • Extra Develop worker helps this Develop phase only.
  • Place-a-die text uses a worker from the named section if available.
  • Reassign moves an unused section die to another normal section.
  • Do not move Spent, Goods, or progress unless text says so.

Cost / Credit

Guide
  • Cost reductions apply when completing a tile.
  • Develop -1 reduces one Development threshold this round.
  • Settle -1 reduces one World threshold this round.
  • Credits finish builds only at completion.
  • Credits are never placed as lasting progress.

Produce / Ship

Guide
  • Produce worker becomes a Good on an eligible World.
  • The Good keeps the worker die color.
  • Good dice are outside the mancala loop.
  • Trade or Consume needs a Ship worker unless text provides one.
  • Shipped Good and Ship worker move to Spent.

Alien / White

Guide
  • Alien means yellow identity, tag, or world type.
  • Alien never means a separate phase section.
  • Yellow in Settle can settle; yellow in Ship can ship.
  • White is flexible but still section-bound.
  • White/yellow do not trigger section match bonuses.

Dice Scoring

Guide
  • Specific-color dice bonuses count physical dice owned.
  • Count sections, Spent, Goods, and construction progress.
  • Military dice means red dice.
  • Alien Technology dice means yellow dice.
  • There is no max-pip track.
