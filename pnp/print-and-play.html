Rival Setup

Solo
  • Use the regular Galaxy mat side for the Rival.
  • Give the Rival 1 Rival Empire card and 1 Difficulty card.
  • Set starting empire level, energy, culture, and VP from the Difficulty card.
  • The Rival uses the same upgrade market as you.

Rival Dice Use

Solo
  • Roll dice by Rival empire level.
  • Resolve dice in the order shown on the Rival Empire card.
  • Skip a die only if it cannot do anything useful.
  • If several dice tie, use the leftmost tied priority first.

Rival Ships

Solo
  • Move a Ship: place a ship on the best planet for its Rival card.
  • If low on resources, it may land on the surface.
  • Otherwise place the ship on the orbit track.
  • Advance Economy/Diplomacy: advance an orbiting ship on a matching colony track.

Rival Growth

Solo
  • Utilize Colony: upgrade empire if affordable.
  • If it cannot upgrade empire, use a colonized planet ability if useful.
  • When it colonizes, score the planet and resolve its Rival Pressure rule.
  • At end of turn, buy the best affordable upgrade if below 3 upgrades.

Upgrade Module

Ref
  • Shuffle the 12 upgrade cards and reveal 3.
  • At end of turn, buy 1 face-up upgrade for 3 energy or 3 culture.
  • Each player may have at most 3 upgrades.
  • Upgrades are first come, first serve.
  • Each upgrade triggers only when you use its die face.

Dice Control

Ref

Converter

If using upgrades, the Converter is available only while you have fewer than 2 upgrades.

Emergency Protocol

If none of your used dice can advance any of your ships on colony tracks, you may spend 1 culture to advance one of those ships 1 space.

Rival Empire Turn

Ref
  • Use the regular Galaxy mat side.
  • Roll dice by Rival empire level.
  • Resolve dice from left to right on the Rival card's Dice Priority.
  • Skip any die that cannot do anything useful.
  • After the Rival turn, it buys the best affordable upgrade from the shared market.
  • When it colonizes, resolve its Pressure rule.
